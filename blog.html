Post 1

During my internship in the field of bioinformatics in Italy, I stayed in a student residence in Grugliasco (near Turin).

It was a great experience and I recommend it!

The monthly amount was around 500 euros (for a single room). Weekly cleaning was done in the rooms, they offer canteen, gym, games and study space available inside the building itself.
